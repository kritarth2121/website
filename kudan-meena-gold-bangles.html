KUNDAN MEENA JEWELLERY

Kundan is a Sanskrit word which means pure sparkling gold or refined gold. In Kundan Jewellery the purest form molten gold is used to set stones. Surana Jewellers jewellery is traditionally handmade and no machines are used in making jewellery. Several articians are involved in creating each individual piece of jewellery. Every piece of kundan jewellery is a team effort with every individual craftperson working on a separate specialized portion. Each Surana Jaipur artisan of the team uses the particular technique that he is skilled at and together they create a beautiful piece of kundan Jewellery.
